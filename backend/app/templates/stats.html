How this fits into the RAG pipeline

When a PDF is uploaded, CiteMind extracts its text, splits it into chunks, converts each chunk into embeddings, stores those vectors in ChromaDB, retrieves relevant chunks for each question, and generates an answer with citations.

1 PDF Upload → Text Extraction
2 Chunking → Embeddings
3 ChromaDB Vector Storage
4 Similarity Search → Cited Answer
